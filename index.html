Travel Tips

Most resorts host events at night with a lot of dancing and drinks. Make sure to enjoy it!

The water bungalows are incredibly popular at Maldives. Regardless of the price, it is definitely something to experience.

There are various activities to do at the beach like: snorkeling, scuba diving, swiming with sharks, etc. Plan your itinerary and book beforehand!
